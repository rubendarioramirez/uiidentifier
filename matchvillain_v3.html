Shape Detection v3 — THRESH_BINARY (White Background)

Pipeline: Grayscale → Blur → Adaptive Threshold (BINARY) → Erode (separate pieces) → Contours → Classify

Key insight: BINARY vs BINARY_INV changes what findContours sees. With THRESH_BINARY, game pieces are white blobs that merge together — adjacent pieces share thin black borders. Without erosion, findContours sees ~5 giant merged shapes. After eroding with a 5×5 kernel (3 iterations), the white blobs shrink and separate, yielding 330 individual contours → 58 classified shapes. The INV version (v2) found 142 shapes because the dark outlines were already naturally separated.
